Your savings are remitted directly to Britam Asset Managers (Kenya) Ltd a Fund Manager licensed and regulated by the Capital Markets Authority.

Product Features

  • Save and borrow against your savings
  • Attractive returns on savings of up to *10% pa.
  • You can borrow up to *twice your savings
  • The more you save, the higher your limit grows
  • Competitive borrowing charges at just 7.5%

How it works

  • Download the Kava App
  • Register. A minimum of KES 10/- Verification Fee will apply
  • Login to the App
  • Create a Kibaba Savings account or select one you have created before
  • Deposit a minimum of KES 1000/- or more - its up to you
  • A minimum period of 24 hours will apply to create your limit
  • Access the Borrow menu on the App
  • Proceed to borrow
  • Your money will be credited to your Mobile Money Account via MPESA
  • Pay back in 30, 60 or 90 Days.
  • As long as you have a loan, you will not be ablate withdraw your savings.
