Welcome to Betsafe Kenya. You are now able to place your bets with us through SMS.

To place your bets, here are the three simple steps;

  1. Simple TEXT the word BETSAFE to 23333
  2. Deposit via M-PESA Paybill 7290099
  3. Text your selection and amount to 23333 e.g Man U Chelsea Arsenal to win 200
